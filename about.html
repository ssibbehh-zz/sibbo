NZ All of Government Service Innovation Lab  

Service Designer
I was Service Designer in the LabPlus team in the All of Government Service Innovation Lab, which is hosted by the Department of Internal Affairs. Working across agencies public and private to help make government services better for New Zealanders.

The Service Innovation Lab provides a space for testing out new concepts for delivery of public services through the lens of life events. They bring together agencies and work with them to identify opportunities, conduct design research, co-design and support the creation of integrated services.

My role as a Service Designer was fast-paced and varied, involving workshop facilitation, user research and testing, design research, creation of designed outputs including UI and UX elements, among many other things.

Learn more about the Service Innovation Lab at https://serviceinnovationlab.github.io/ and https://www.digital.govt.nz/blog/tag/service-innovation-lab
